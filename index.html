Biography

Combining my passion for problem solving & my experience streamlining operations, I develop clean, custom web experiences for the modern world.

I have spent my entire adult life creating systems. I enjoy discovering even the slightest of improvements. I consider well-organized, complex structures a thing of pure beauty. "Work smart" is not solely my professional philosophy— this mantra embodies my approach to life—and is realized in the combination of my passion for building and problem solving to address issues of inefficiency, resource waste, and corporate stagnation. I am very comfortable with technical concepts of varying complexities. Whatever I don't know, I am eager to learn.

I spent the past year at a premier digital marketing firm in Culver City, honing my skills in SEO analysis, strategy, and technical implementation, conversion rate optimization, SEO campaign lifecycle/management, and finally the intricacies of overall SEO campaign strategy and keyword research. Having learned SEO via this immersive agency experience, I'm comfortable speaking to SEO issues and strategies across a broad range of industries.

As an account manager at Powered Office, I managed a portfolio of twenty accounts, ushering them through our development process from conception through development and finally deployment. Before my promotion to a Digital Account Manager, I managed a team of seven Customer Success Professionals as a Supervisor within the Customer Service department where I restructured internal processes, and implemented new processes, systems & tools to augment employee productivity by 45%, reduce resource waste and minimize corporate liability.

Countless projects later, I'm still obsessed as ever with problem solving, especially where process "red-tape" bars agency efficacy and achievement. I reside in sunny Los Angeles and work in tech, both professionally and as a hobby. I'm developing my already keen appreciation for design and sharpening my graphic design chops for fun as well these days. I am an avid wanderlust, and in down-time enjoy listening to live music, playing guitar, and hiking nature trails when time allows.
