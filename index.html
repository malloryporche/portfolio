Biography

Creating my life's work by weaving my passion for technology and experience in streamlining operations and improving organizational efficiency.

I have spent my entire adult life creating systems. I enjoy discovering even the slightest of process improvements. I consider well organized, complex structures a thing of pure beauty. "Work smart" is not solely my professional philosophy— this mantra embodies my approach to life—the discovery that I can combine my passion for building and solving problems of efficacy has changed my life. I am very comfortable with technical concepts of varying complexities. Whatever I don't know, I am eager to learn.

I spent the past year at a premier web design and digital marketing firm in Culver City where I honed my skills in SEO analysis, strategy and implementation, conversion rate optimization, campaign management, and keyword research.

As an account manager at Powered Office, I managed a portfolio of twenty accounts, ushering them through our development process from conception through to creation. Before my promotion to a Tier II Account Manager, I was the Supervisor for the Customer Service department reporting directly to the COO, where I restructured our systems and processes, and implemented new systems amp& tools to augment employee productivity, reduce resource waste and corporate liability via Google Apps.

Countless projects and numerous ups-and-downs later, I'm still as obsessed as ever with problem solving, productivity and agency acheivement.

I reside in sunny Los Angeles and work in tech, both professionally and as a hobby. I'm also developing a keen appreciation for good design and sharpening my graphic design chops for fun these days. I am a superior wanderlust, and in my down-time enjoy playing guitar, hiking in Malibu, and learning new languages when time allows.
