Biography

Creating my life's work by combining my passion for problem solving and technology and experience in streamlining operations and improving organizational efficiency. I also make custom-crafted web applications for the modern world.

I have spent my entire adult life creating systems. I enjoy discovering even the slightest of process improvements. I consider well organized, complex structures a thing of pure beauty. "Work smart" is not solely my professional philosophy— this mantra embodies my approach to life—the discovery that I can combine my passion for building and solving problems of efficacy has changed my life. I am very comfortable with technical concepts of varying complexities. Whatever I don't know, I am eager to learn.

I spent this past year at a premier digital marketing firm in Culver City where I honed my skills in SEO analysis, strategy and implementation, conversion rate optimization, SEO campaign management, and keyword research.

As an account manager at Powered Office, I managed a portfolio of twenty accounts, ushering them through our development process from conception through to development and finally deployment. Before my promotion to a Digital Account Manager, I oversaw a team of seven Customer Success Professionals in the Customer Service department where I restructured internal processes, and implemented new systems & tools to augment employee productivity by 45%, reduce resource waste and minimize corporate liability via the Google Apps Suite.

Countless projects later, I'm still obsessed as ever with problem solving, especially where process "red-tape" bars agency efficacy and achievement. I reside in sunny Los Angeles and work in tech, both professionally and as my hobby. I'm developing my already keen appreciation for good design and sharpening my graphic design chops for fun as well these days. I am an avid wanderlust, and in down-time enjoy listening to live music, playing guitar, and hiking nature trails when time allows.
